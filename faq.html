Frequently Asked Questions

What is Helvora

Helvora is a digital healthcare platform designed to connect patients with doctors, schedule appointments, access medical records, and receive virtual consultations.

How does Helvora work?

Users can create an account, search for doctors based on specialty, book appointments (in-person or virtual), and access healthcare resources conveniently.

Can I share my records with another doctor?

Yes, you can grant access to your chosen healthcare provider for better diagnosis and treatment.

Is Helvora free to use?

Creating an account and browsing doctors is free, but consultation and other premium services may have associated costs.

Is my personal information safe?

Absolutely. Helvora follows strict security measures and complies with healthcare data protection regulations.

How do I book an appointment?

Creating an account and browsing doctors is free, but consultation and other premium services may have associated costs.

How does Helvora verify doctors before they can offer services on the platform?

Doctors are verified through license submission, credential checks, and approval by our medical review team

Can I access my medical records on Helvora?

Yes, your medical history, prescriptions, and lab results are securely stored and accessible via your dashboard.

Is Helvora available on mobile devices?

Yes, Helvora is accessible via web and mobile devices for convenience.

Can I switch doctors if I’m not satisfied?

Yes, you can search for and book appointments with other doctors at any time.

Our mission is to revolutionize the way people connect with healthcare providers, manage their health and access critical information, making quality healthcare more accessible to everyone.
